      HOOPIUM has one single mission: scanning Solana tokens for rug vibes
      before you ape. Trained on thousands of hours of market data from
      the trenches. Paste a contract address (CA) in the terminal and get
      hard data, red flags, a 0-100 HOOPIUM score, a read on whether the
      coin's narrative fits the current market meta, and an instant
      verdict against your own buy rules. Drop an
      @username instead and HOOPIUM digs through Wayback Machine
      archives for the account's provable age, tweet history and rebrands.
      Zero financial advice — dyor, ser.

on-chain checks

        
Mint & freeze authority and top-10 holder concentration, straight from the chain.

      

      

        
market sanity

        
Liquidity vs mcap, 24h volume, pair age and bonding curve status. Dead token? Easy rug? It'll say so.

      

      

        
cross-referenced

        
An independent risk score plus 3-5 similar-named tokens, so you see how the clones ended.

      

      

        
meta radar

        
Reads what's trending on pump.fun right now and judges if this coin's lore fits the meta.

      

      

        
x account history

        
Drop an @username and HOOPIUM checks Wayback Machine archives: confirmed minimum account age, tweet count then vs now, and a flag if a mass deletion shows up.

      

      

        
chrome extension

        
A mini scanner in your toolbar — copy a CA on your trading platform, click, scan. install it here.

      

      

        
0% financial advice

        
The gut take is ML pattern recognition with personality, never a buy signal. NFA by design, dyor ser.
