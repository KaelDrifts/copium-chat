COPIUM

solana token risk scanner — paste a CA, get the truth

Drop any Solana contract address into the terminal and COPIUM pulls on-chain data, liquidity, holders, pump.fun status and RugCheck, runs the red-flag heuristics and writes you a risk report with a score. It won't tell you what to buy — it'll tell you what smells like a rug.

> launch terminal_

cat about.txt

COPIUM has one single mission: scanning Solana tokens for rug vibes before you ape. Paste a contract address (CA) in the terminal and it checks DexScreener, the Solana chain, pump.fun and RugCheck — all free APIs.

The report covers mint & freeze authority, top-10 holder concentration, liquidity vs market cap, volume, bonding curve status and how similar-named tokens performed, plus a 0-100 COPIUM score computed with plain heuristics. And you can set your own buy rules (market cap, holders, whatever threshold you want) — every scan checks them and gives you an instant WOULD BUY / WOULD NOT BUY verdict by your own criteria.

What it does not do: financial advice, price predictions, trading signals. The gut take at the end is an automated opinion — always dyor, ser.

ls -la specs/

on-chain checks

Mint & freeze authority status and top-10 holder concentration, straight from the public Solana RPC. No paid APIs.

market sanity

Liquidity vs market cap, 24h volume, pair age and pump.fun bonding curve status. Dead token? Easy rug? It'll say so.

cross-referenced

RugCheck's score plus 3-5 similar-named tokens from DexScreener, so you see how clones of this play usually end.

0% financial advice

The report ends with an automated gut take, clearly labeled as heuristics — never a buy signal. NFA by design, dyor ser.

set your own thresholds — every scan checks them and gives you an instant WOULD BUY / WOULD NOT BUY verdict on top of the COPIUM score. saved in this browser.

coded rules are written in Python: numbers, + - * / %, comparisons (including chained, e.g. 100000 <= market_cap_usd <= 1000000), and/or/not, parentheses, and these variables: copium_score, market_cap_usd, liquidity_usd, liquidity_to_mcap_pct, volume_24h_usd, price_usd, price_change_24h_pct, pair_age_days, top10_holders_pct, total_holders, rugcheck_score, bonding_curve_pct, mint_renounced, freeze_renounced.

⚠ NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE. COPIUM's reports and gut takes are automated heuristics, nothing else.

No trades executed, no price predictions. Data from free public APIs, may be wrong or stale. dyor, ser.
