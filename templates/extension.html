Don't leave your trading platform to check a token. The HOOPIUM Chrome extension puts the scanner one click away from your toolbar: copy a CA on Axiom, Photon, BullX, DexScreener or pump.fun, open the popup, paste, scan.

Even faster: select the CA on any page → right click → "Scan with HOOPIUM" — the popup opens with the report already loading. Your buy rules work there too, so you get your WOULD BUY / WOULD NOT BUY verdict mid-trade, in seconds.

> download extension (.zip)
  1. click the button above and unzip the download
  2. open chrome://extensions and enable Developer mode
  3. click Load unpacked and pick the unzipped hoopium-extension folder
  4. pin HOOPIUM to your toolbar and start scanning, ser
